Our History

our principles

Mariakani Secondary School was founded in 1968 a joint harambee day and mixed school. It has since evolved into a five streamed boys boarding school in forms I & II and three streams form III & IV. The school has 752 students and 32 teachers

location

Our School is located a few kilometers away the heart of the city centre that has been designed for the need of both learning and talent.

our mission

To be the preferred center that produces well balanced graduates.
